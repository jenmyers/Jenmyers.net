I’m a technologist and writer in Chicago.

I've been making things on the web since 2000, and I have been involved in tech education and diversity/inclusion advocacy for several years—including as a chapter founder, teacher and leadership community manager of Girl Develop It, and the founder of Code and Cupcakes Chicago. I have also spoken extensively about web development/design, diversity and education. I currently work as the director of open source curriculum at Pluralsight.
